Help - Registration Guide

Registration Guide

Step 1: Personal Information

Fill in your basic details including name, email, and location. Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*).

Step 2: Your Craft

Tell us about your passion or craft. Be specific about what you do and what inspires you.

Step 3: Showcase Image

Upload a high-quality image that represents your work or shows you in action. This will be displayed in our community collage.

Tips for Success

  • Use a clear, high-resolution image
  • Write a compelling bio that tells your story
  • Include your social media handles for networking
  • Be authentic and passionate about your craft
